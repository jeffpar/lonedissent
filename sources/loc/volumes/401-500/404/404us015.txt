PER CURIAM.
On this appeal we postponed the question of jurisdiction pending the hearing of the case on the merits.
401 U. S. 906 (1971).
Having heard the case on its merits, we find that this
Court does have jurisdiction (Alabama Teachers v. Alabama Public School and College Authority, 393 U. S. 400
(1969)) and affirm the judgmentof the District Court
for the Northern District of California. Johnson v.
Avery, 393 U. S. 483 (1969).
