The court will not, on the motion of the plaintiff in error, Instruct the clrcult cV1urt
to permit him to withdraw his demurrer after an affirmance of the judgment
of the circuit court; although this might have been done had the judgment
been reversed.

Mr Swann, of counsel for the plaintiff in error in this cause,
moved the court to amend the judgment entered in fhis cause,
by instructing the court below to permit him to withdraw his
demurrer: on consideration whereof, this court is of opinion
that although this might have been done upon a reversal, yet
it cannot be done where the judgment of the court has been
affirmed, as this court cannot disaffirm its judgment.
Whereupon, it is ordered by the court, that the said motion be and the same is hereby overruled.
