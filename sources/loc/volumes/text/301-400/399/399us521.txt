HOCKER, WARDEN v. HEFFLEY
ON

PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED
STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE NINTH CIRCUIT

No. 1195.

Decided June 29, 1970

Certiorari granted; 420 F. 2d 881, vacated and remanded.
PER CURIAM.

The motion of the respondent for leave to proceed
in forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari
are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is
remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the
Ninth Circuit for further consideration in light of Chambers v. Maroney, ante, p. 42.
MR. JUSTICE HARLAN is of the opinion that certiorari
should be denied. However, the case having been taken
for review, he would affirm the judgment below for the
reasons stated in his separate opinion in Chambers v.
Maroney, ante, p. 55.
