PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and
the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment of the United States Court of Appeals for the
District of Columbia Circuit is vacated and the case is

and in light of Hill v. United States, 356 U. S. 704, decided
June 2, 1958.
