PER CURIAM.

Upon the consent of the Solicitor General and consideration of the entire record, the petition for a writ of
certiorari is granted, the judgment of the United States
Court of Appeals for the Sixth Circuit is vacated and the
cause is remanded to that court with instructions to
remand it to the United States Tax Court for computation and imposition of civil fraud penalty in accordance
with the provisions of Public Law 89-359, 80 Stat. 28.
