No. 163.

Decided October-13, 1958.

George V. Powell for appellant.
John J. O'Connell, Attorney General of Washington,
and Robert L. Simpson, Assistant Attorney General, for

PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to file brief of Berkshire Hathaway, Inc., as amicus curiae, is denied. The motion to
dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed for want
of a substantial federal question.
