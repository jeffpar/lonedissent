OCTOBER TERM, 1958.
Per Curiam.

359 U. S.

HANDFORD v. UNITED STATES.
ON

PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED
STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE FIFTH CIRCUIT.

No. 578.

Decided March 23, 1959.

Certiorari granted; judgment reversed upon consideration of the
entire record and the confession of error by the Solicitor General.
Reported below: 260 F. 2d 890.

Julian Webb for petitioner.
Solicitor General Rankin for the United States.
PER CURIAM.

The petition for writ of certiorari is granted. Upon
consideration of the entire record and the confession of
error by the Solicitor General, the judgment of the United
States Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit is reversed.
