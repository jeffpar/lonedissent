PER CURIAM.
The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperisand
the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the Supreme
Court of Florida for a hearing on the issue of whether
petitioner was denied the assistance of counsel at the time
he entered his plea of guilty.
