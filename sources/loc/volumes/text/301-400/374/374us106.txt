OCTOBER TERM, 1962.
Per Curiam.

374 U. S.

BENTLEY v. ALASKA.
ON PETITION

FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE SUPREME
COURT OF ALASKA.

No. 647, Misc. Decided June 10, 1963.
Certiorari granted; judgment vacated; and case remanded.

Petitioner pro se.
George N. Hayes, Attorney General of Alaska, and
John K. Brubaker, Assistant Attorney General, for
respondent.
PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and
the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the Supreme
Court of Alaska for reconsideration in light of Douglas
v. California, 372 U. S. 353, Lane v. Brown, 372 U. S.
477, and Draperv. Washington, 372 U. S. 487.
