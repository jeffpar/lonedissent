PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and
the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment of the Supreme Court of California is vacated and
the case is remanded for further consideration in light of
Ker v. California,ante, p. 23, and McDonald v. United
States, 335 U. S. 451.
MR. JUSTICE HARLAN

concurs in the result.
