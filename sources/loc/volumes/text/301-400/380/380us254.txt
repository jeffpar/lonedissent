Vernon Crawford, Morton Stavis, Martin R. Bradley,

PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and
the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The
Court is of the view that on the record the petitioner is
an indigent. Therefore, the judgment must be reversed.
Griffin v. Illinois, 351 U. S. 12.
