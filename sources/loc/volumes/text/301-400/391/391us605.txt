DECISIONS PER CURIAM.
391 U. S.

June 3, 1968.

BONNIE v. GLALJDEN, WARDEN.
ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED
STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE NINTH CIRCUIT.

No. 731, Misc. Decided June 3, 1968.
Certiorari granted; 377 F. 2d 555, vacated and remanded.

Howard R. Lcmergan for petitioner.
Robert Y. Thornton, Attorney General of Oregon,
and David H. Blunt, Assistant Attorney General, for
respondent.
PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis
and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted.
The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to
the United States Court of. Appeals for the Ninth Circuit
for further consideration in light of Carafa v. LaVallee,
ante, p. 234.
