FOLLETTE, WARDEN v. COMACHO
ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED
STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE SECOND CIRCUIT

No. 1394.

Decided June 1, 1970

Certiorari granted; 421 F. 2d 822, vacated and remanded.
PER CURIAM.

The motion of the respondent for leave to proceed in
forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari
are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is
remanded to the Court of Appeals for further consideration in light of McMann v. Richardson, 397 U. S. 759.
MR. JUSTICE MARSHALL took no part in the consideration or decision of this case.
