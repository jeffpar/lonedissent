DECISIONS PER CURIAM.
381 U.S.

May 17, 1965.

O'CONNELL ET AL. V. MANNING ET Al.
CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR
THE SECOND CIRCUIT.

No. 454. Decided May 17, 1965.*
Judgment vacated and cases remanded to District Court with directions to dismiss complaint as moot.
Reported below: 329 F. 2d 11.

Martin C. Seham for petitioners in No. 454.
Arthur M. Wisehart for petitioner in No. 455.
Asher W. Schwartz and Walter N. Kaufman for
respondents in both cases.
PER CURIAM.

The motions of the petitioners to vacate are granted.
The judgment of the United States Court of Appeals for
the Second Circuit is vacated and the cases are remanded
to the United States District Court for the Southern District of New York with directions to dismiss the complaint
as moot.
MR. JUSTICE GOLDBERG

took no part in the considera-

tion or decision of these cases.

*Together with No. 455, American Airlines, Inc. v. Manning et al.,
also on certiorari to the same court.
