TURNEY v. ARKANSAS.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF ARKANSAS.

No. 967.

Decided May 17, 1965.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.
Reported below: 237 Ark. 569, 375 S. W. 2d 375.

Thomas J. Gentry for appellant.
Bruce Bennett, Attorney General of Arkansas, and
Richard B. Adkisson, Chief Assistant Attorney General,
for appellee.
PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed for want of jurisdiction. Treating the papers
whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of
certiorari, certiorari is denied.
