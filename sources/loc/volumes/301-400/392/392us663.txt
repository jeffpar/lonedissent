PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and
the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The
judgment is vacated and the case is remanded for a hearing as provided in Sims v. Georgia, 385 U. S. 538, and
Jackson v. Denno, 378 U. S. 368.
MR. JUSTICE DOUGLAS is of the opinion that the petitionfor a writ of certiorari should be granted and the case
set for oral argument.
