STEWART ET AL. v. INDUSTRIAL COMMISSION

APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF ILLINOIS.

No. 1034.

Decided April 17, 1967.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.

Bernard Kleiman, Gilbert A. Cornfield, Gilbert Feldman, Jason Gesmer and Alton Sharpe for appellants.

PER CURIAM.

dismissed for want of jurisdiction.

a petition for a writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
