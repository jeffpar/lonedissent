PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and
the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The
judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the
United States District Court for the District of Columbia
for further consideration in light of Kaufman v. United
States, ante, p. 217.
MR. JUSTICE FORTAS concurs in the disposition of this
case but notes that the Court's action does not imply
any view as to the merits of any of the petitioner's claims
including his assertions as to double jeopardy.
