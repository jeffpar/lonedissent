PER CURIAM.

The petition for writ of certiorari to the Court of
Appeals for the Sixth Circuit is granted, and the judgment dismissing petitioner's appeal to that court is
reversed. The time limited by 28 U. S. C. ยง 2107 and
Fed. Rule Civ. Proc. 73 for the filing of the notice of
appeal from the judgment appealed from was 30 days.
However, Fed. Rule Civ. Proc. 6 (a), as amended, provides that in computing the period, "[t]he last day of the
period so computed shall be included, unless it is a Saturday, a Sunday, or a legal holiday, in which event the
period runs until the end of the next day which is not a
'Saturday, a Sunday, or a legal holiday." Since the thir-

tieth day following entry of the judgment appealed from
was Saturday and the notice of appeal was filed the following Monday, we hold that the filing of the notice of
appeal was timely. The provision of Rule 6 (a) was not
