the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment of the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit is vacated and the case is
remanded to that court for consideration in light of Ellis
v. United States, 356 U. .S. 674. It is ordered that the
judgment in this case issue forthwith.
