WINTERS v. OHIO.
ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE SUPREME
COURT OF OHIO.

No. 388, Misc.

Decided June 25, 1962.

Certiorari granted; judgment vacated; case remanded for further
proceedings.

Petitioner pro se.
Richard 0. Harris for respondent.
PER CURIAM.

The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and
the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the Supreme
Court of Ohio for further proceedings. Griffin v. Illinois,
351 U. S. 12.
MR. JUSTICE FRANKFURTER

took no part in the con-

sideration or decision of this case.
