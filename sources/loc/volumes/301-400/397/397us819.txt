DECISIONS PER CURIAM
397 U. S.

May 4, 1970

TEX TAN WELHAUSEN CO. ET AL. v. NATIONAL
LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
ON

PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED
STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR THE FIFTH CIRCUIT

No. 1288. Decided May 4, 1970
Certiorari granted; 419 F. 2d 1265, vacated and remanded.

Theo. F. Weiss for petitioners.
Solicitor General Griswold, Arnold Ordman, Dominick L. Manoli, Norton J. Come, and Linda Sher for
respondent.
L. N. D. Wells, Jr., for Amalgamated Meat Cutters
& Butcher Workmen of North America, AFL-CIO,
intervenor below.
PER CURIAM.

The petition for a writ of certiorari is granted, the
judgment is vacated, and the case is remanded to the
United States Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit
for further consideration in light of H. K. Porter Co.
v. National Labor Relations Board, ante, p. 99.
MR. JusTICE DOUGLS dissents to the remand.
