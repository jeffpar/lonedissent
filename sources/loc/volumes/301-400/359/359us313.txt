DECISIONS PER CURIAM.
April 20, 1959.

359 U. S.

FORE v. TOTH.
APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF OHIO.

No. 740.

Decided April 20, 1959.

Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.
Reported below: 168 Ohio St. 363, 155 N. E. 2d 194.

Maurice F. Hanning and J. King Rosendale for
appellant.
Charles C. Redmond for appellee.
Jack P. F. Gremillion, Attorney General of Louisiana,
filed a brief for the State of Louisiana, as amicus curiae,
in support of appellant.
PER CURIAM.

The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is
dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was
taken as a petition for certiorari, certiorari is denied.
MR. JUSTICE STEWART

took no part in the consideration

or decision of this case.
