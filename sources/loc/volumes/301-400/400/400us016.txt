.PER CURIAM.

The petition for a writ of certiorari is granted, the
judgment is vacated, and the case is remanded to the
,-Court of Appeals of New York for further consideration
in light 'of Waller v. Florida, 397 U. S. 387.
is of the opinion that certiorari
should be granted and the judgment reversed on the
ground that the 'state prosecution is barred by the
Double Jeopardy Clause of the Fifth Amendment of
the Constitution.
